About Artist

Who is Sa-Young?

Sa-Young is an Afro-fusion artist that makes groovy-music with intention. His sound sits at the edge of Afrobeat and Alté, a style which he calls Afro-Twist.

The Sound

Rooted in percussive groove and shaped by storytelling lyrics, Afro-Twist is built for both dancing and reflection.

The Return

After a decade away, Sa-Young returned with clarity. No noise. No rush. Just clean, vibrant music and two impactful EPs within two years.

Stage Presence

Performed alongside icons like Kizz Daniel, Chike, Buju, and headlined Blastfest Seattle with Adekunle Gold and Odumodublvck.

Now performing with The Tribe Band, delivering a live experience with energy and musicianship.
